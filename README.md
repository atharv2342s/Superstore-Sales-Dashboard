#  Super Store Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

##  Project Overview

The **Super Store Sales Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze sales, profit, orders, and shipping performance of a retail superstore. The dashboard provides clear insights into **regional performance, customer segments, payment modes, shipping methods, and product categories**.

This project is ideal for **business stakeholders and data analysts** to monitor performance and identify growth opportunities.

---

##  Project Objectives

* Track **overall sales, profit, orders, and shipping days**
* Analyze **monthly sales and profit trends**
* Compare **regional and state-wise performance**
* Understand **customer segments and payment preferences**
* Evaluate **shipping modes and product categories**

---

##  Dashboard KPIs

* **Total Sales:** 1.6M
* **Total Profit:** 175K
* **Total Orders:** 22K
* **Average Shipping Days:** 4

---

##  Key Dashboard Features

###  Interactive Filters

* **Region Selector:** Central, East, South, West
* **Year Comparison:** 2019 vs 2020

---

###  Visual Insights

#### 1. Sales by Payment Mode

* COD, Online, and Card payments
* COD contributes the highest share of sales

#### 2. Sales & Profit by Month

* Month-wise comparison for **2019 and 2020**
* Identifies seasonal trends and peak months

#### 3. Sales by Ship Mode

* Standard Class generates the highest sales
* Same Day shipping has the lowest contribution

#### 4. Sales by Region & State

* West region leads in total sales
* Map visual provides state-level performance analysis

#### 5. Sales by Segment

* Consumer segment dominates sales
* Corporate and Home Office follow

#### 6. Sales by Category & Sub-Category

* Office Supplies lead among categories
* Phones, Chairs, and Binders are top sub-categories
